## Description (260 chars)
```
TrustLayer wraps around any AI you use — Ollama, Claude, GPT, Gemini — and adds trust scores, hallucination detection, cost tracking, and model comparison. Local-first. No account needed. Open source.
```

## Maker Comment (first comment after launch — this gets pinned)
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm the creator of TrustLayer, and I built this because I kept getting burned by AI outputs I couldn't verify.

The problem that drove me here: I use 4-5 different AI tools. Every one of them confidently says wrong things sometimes. None of them tell me when. None of them show me what I'm spending across all of them. And I have no way to know which one is actually best for my specific tasks.

TrustLayer fixes all of that in one open source tool that runs locally:

🔍 **Trust Scoring** — Every AI response gets a 0-100 score. "87% verified. 2 claims flagged."

🧠 **Hallucination Detection** — Flags responses where claims can't be sourced or seem fabricated

💰 **Cost Tracking** — Real-time spend across all your providers with budget alerts

📊 **Model Comparison** — Test YOUR actual tasks across models side-by-side

📚 **Offline Knowledge Base** — Index your docs and use AI completely offline with Ollama

🔧 **No-Code Workflows** — Visual builder for automating repetitive AI tasks

Everything runs on your machine. No telemetry. No accounts. One command install.

Would love to hear feedback — especially from people building with local AI!
```
